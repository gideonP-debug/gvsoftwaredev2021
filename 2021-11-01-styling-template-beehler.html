Software Dev - Cloud Computing:

  • On-demand availability of computer system resources, especially data storage and computing power.
  • No direct active management of servers - no updating or patching by the users.
  • Store and access data and programs over the internet instead of your computers hard drive.
  1. Advantages of cloud computing:
    • Back-up and restore data
    • Improved collaboration
    • Excellent accessibility
    • Low maintenance cost
    • Mobility
    • IServices in the pay-per-use model
    • Unlimited storage capacity
    • Data security
  2. Disadvantages of cloud computing:
    • Internet Connectivity
    • Vendor lock-in
    • Limited Control
    • Security

Software Dev - Agile Methodology:

  • A structured and iterative approach to project management and product development.
  • Provides a methodology for self-organizing teams to respond to change quickly.
  • Provides continuous feedback to verify the team is building the right thing and building it right.
  • Allows use of different strategies to accomplish the work.
  1. SCRUM:
    • Requires Defined Roles
      • Product Owner
      • SCRUM Master
      • Development Team
    • Fixed Cadence - Sprint
    • Code release at end of each Sprint
    • Team should not make changes during the Sprint
  2. Kanban:
    • Continuous flow
    • Continuous delivery
    • No required roles
    • Change can happen at any time
